About

Born and raised in central Newfoundland Natasha has always had a fondness for animals. Although when she was younger she wanted to be a veternarian she found a different way to show them love and compassion, through pampering and grooming. After moving to labrador city in her 20s she soon found it a cozy place to call home and have a family. Now taking up permanent residence in Labrador she is owner and operator at Big Land Pet Pawlor where she aims to care for your pets!
